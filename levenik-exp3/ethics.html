Briefing

The study is being conducted by Glenn Williams, Nikolay Panayotov and Vera Kempe, has received ethical approval by the Research Ethics Committee of Abertay University Dundee, Scotland, and complies with the data protection measures specified by GDPR.

It will involve one session and will typically last around 1hr 30mins. For the learning to be successful, we ask you to complete the task in one sitting. We will give you the opportunity to take some short breaks between the different parts.

Your participation in this study is voluntary. Compensation will be awarded after completion of the study if you provide honest answers within one testing session.

There are no known risks for you in this study.

You may decide to stop taking part at any time during the study without explanation and without penalty. To do so, simply close your browser at any point. If you do, your data will be destroyed, and will not be included in any analyses or publications.

Any personally identifying information related to this study (e.g. your Prolific ID) will be kept separately from your responses with an anonymised identifier . Your responses to this study (including written responses and recordings) will only be associated with an anonymised identifier. As your data is kept anonymous, we will not be able to remove it after completion of the study. We will not analyse individual data, only the group data. All of the data associated with this study will be stored on password-protected servers to which only the researchers will have access. Your anonymised data will be uploaded to an open, online repository after the completion of testing. However, we will not share your personally-identifiable data, such as your voice recordings, on any open repositories. Instead, we will only host transcriptions of your voice recordings (i.e. a written record of what you said) on the open online repositories.
