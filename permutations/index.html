Magic Word Permutations

Please input a word construction adhering to English phonotactics:

  • Words must contain at least 1 vowel
  • Words can have a maximum of 3 consonants in an onset, and 4 in a coda.
    Note: Our site does not control for English phonotactics for words with greater than 2 consonant clusters.

Important: Because of combinatorical explosion this is very slow for long constructions, such as CCVCCVCC

Note: Words cannot include sequences of repeated consonants and are thus omitted from the output.
